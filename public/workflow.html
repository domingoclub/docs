We make this list to share the tools, devices, services and softwares we have chosen to work with and why. We truly believe that there are good alternatives to the GAFAM products and that there is a need to be more aware of what we daily use and who we choose to support.

Softwares

3D modeler

  • FreeCAD

We use FreeCAD to design our project because it is an open-source parametric 3D modeler. Parametric modeling allows you to easily modify your design by going back into your model history and changing its parameters.
